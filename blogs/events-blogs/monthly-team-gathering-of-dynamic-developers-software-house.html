Dynamic Developers Team Gathering - Best Software House

“When there is team work and collaboration, wonderful things can be achieved”

Dynamic Developers is amongst the top most Software Houses in Rahim Yar Khan and is responsible for creating the products that are useful for customers and organizations. On 11 December, 2021 a team meetup of Dynamic Developers was held in Canal View Hotel and Restaurant Rahim Yar Khan.

Mr. Hafiz Muhammad Arslan Chief Executive Officer, Dynamic Developers hosted this meetup. A special thanks to Mr. and Mrs. Engineer Muhammad Kamran for their kind appearance.

Agenda of the meeting:

The development of the company was the main agenda that was raised in this meetup. Mr. Hafiz Muhammad Arslan gave his ideas on this subject. Further, Many team members including Ms. Kinza Tayyab, Ms. Maria Sehar, Mr. Abu-Bakr, Mr. Haris Abdullah actively played their part in the brainstorming session coming up with different perspectives and suggestions that gave a good turn to his subject. The humble guidance by Mr. Muhammad Kamran (Member Board of Directors) enlightened this session. Ms. Kinza Tayyab presented a complete marketing plan. Moreover, some other strategies were also discussed that could play a vital role in companies development. Ms. Maria Sehar shared some of the ideas that can be beneficial in the improvement of Company’s Management.

EMPLOY OF THE MONTH:

Our Company values the team members. We guide our team members at their bad and we always appreciate them at their best. Therefore, Today we presented the Employ of the Month award to Ms. Maria Sehar based on her proportion of hardwork for the Company's progress.

In the end, Team Dynamic Developers arranged a lunch for team members and especially for our Mentors Mr. and Mrs. Muhammad Kamran.
